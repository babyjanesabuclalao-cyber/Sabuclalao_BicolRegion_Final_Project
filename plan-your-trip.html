3-Day Suggested Itinerary

Day Activities
Day 1 Arrival, City Tour, Cagsawa Ruins
Day 2 Mayon Volcano ATV, Local Food Tour
Day 3 Beach/Island Hopping, Departure

Travel Tips

  • Always carry water and snacks.
  • Wear comfortable shoes.
  • Keep your valuables safe.
  • Check weather before packing.
  • Plan transportation in advance.

Packing Checklist

  • Comfortable clothing
  • Sunblock and hat
  • Reusable water bottle
  • Travel documents
  • Phone, charger, and power bank
  • MONEY
