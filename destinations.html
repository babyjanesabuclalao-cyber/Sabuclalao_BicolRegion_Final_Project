Albay

Mayon Volcano

Amazing volcano with a perfect cone shape. Visitors enjoy sightseeing, trekking, and taking breathtaking photos.
Location: Legazpi City, Albay

Cagsawa Ruins

Ruins of a church destroyed by Mayon’s 1814 eruption, now a historical park. Offers stunning views of the volcano.
Location: Daraga, Albay

Sumlang Lake

A peaceful lake with bamboo rafts and a relaxing view of Mayon Volcano. Great for leisure and photography.
Location: Camalig, Albay

Lignon Hill Nature Park

A hilltop park perfect for ziplining, sightseeing, and panoramic views of Legazpi City and Mayon.
Location: Legazpi City, Albay

Daraga Church

Historic baroque church with a hilltop view of Mayon Volcano. Popular among tourists and pilgrims.
Location: Daraga, Albay

Kawa-Kawa Hill and Natural Park

Known for its life-size Stations of the Cross and “cauldron-shaped” hill. A popular pilgrimage site.
Location: Ligao City, Albay

Hoyop-Hoyopan Cave

A natural cave with wind passages and historical stories, once used as WWII shelter.
Location:Camalig, Albay

Vera Falls

A hidden mountain waterfall with clear, cool waters. Perfect for swimming and nature trips.
Location: Malinao, Albay

Camarines Norte

Calaguas Islands

Known for white-sand beaches and crystal-clear waters. Perfect for camping and relaxation.
Location: Vinzons, Camarines Norte

Bagasbas Beach

A long beach famous for surfing and recreational activities.
Location: Daet, Camarines Norte

Mercedes Group of Islands

A cluster of scenic islands ideal for snorkeling, swimming, and island hopping.
Location: Mercedes, Camarines Norte

Apuao Grande Islands

Known for peaceful atmosphere, mini-forests, and long sandbars.
Location: Mercedes, Camarines Norte

Pulang Daga Beach

Unique reddish-sand beach with clear water, ideal for swimming.
Location: Paracale, Camarines Norte

Mampurog River

URefreshing river with picnic spots and natural scenery.
Location: San Lorenzo Ruiz, Camarines Norte

Mananap Falls

Calm waterfall with natural pools for swimming and relaxation.
Location: San Vicente, Camarines Norte

Capalonga Shrine

Pilgrimage site known for the miraculous image of Our Lady of Candelaria.
Location: Capalonga, Camarines Norte

Camarines Sur

Caramoan Islands

Famous for beaches, lagoons, and limestone cliffs. Featured in *Survivor*.
Location: Caramoan, Camarines Sur

CamSur Watersports Complex

World-class wakeboarding and watersports park.
Location: Pili, Camarines Sur

Mt. Isarog National Park

Protected park with waterfalls, wildlife, and trekking trails.
Location:Naga City & Pili, Camarines Sur

Malabsay Falls

Refreshing waterfall beneath Mt. Isarog.
Location:Naga City, Camarines Sur

Naga Metropolitan Cathedral

Historical cathedral with classic architecture.
Location:Naga City, Camarines Sur

Basilica of Our Lady of Peñafrancia

Home of Bicol’s beloved Patroness. Millions visit during the annual feast.
Location:Naga City, Camarines Sur

Panicuason Hot Springs

Natural hot springs surrounded by forest. Perfect for relaxing.
Location:Naga City, Camarines Sur

Sabang Beach

Popular beach and surfing spot; jump-off point to Caramoan.
Location:San Jose, Camarines Sur

Catanduanes

Binurong Point

Cliffside viewpoint overlooking the sea. Best for sunrise and photos.
Location: Baras, Catanduanes

Puraran Beach

Famous for surfing waves, also called the “Majestic Wave.”
Location: Baras, Catanduanes

Twin Rock Beach

Family-friendly beach with interesting rock formations.
Location: Virac, Catanduanes

Maribina Falls

A refreshing waterfall surrounded by nature.
Location:Bato, Catanduanes

Bato Church

Historic coral-stone church, symbol of Catanduanes’ faith.
Location:Bato, Catanduanes

Balacay Point

Scenic viewpoint with panoramic ocean views.
Location: Baras, Catanduanes

Igang Beach

White-sand beach with calm and clear waters.
Location:Virac, Catanduanes

Luyang Cave

Cave with historical and religious significance.
Location: San Andres, Catanduanes

Masbate

Burias Island

Beautiful island with beaches and rock formations, great for island hopping.
Location:San Pascual, Masbate

Sombrero Island

Hat-shaped island with clear waters, ideal for swimming and snorkeling.
Location:Burias, Masbate

Tinalisayan Island

Known for its long sandbar and crystal-clear waters.
Location:Burias, Masbate

Buntod Reef Marine Sanctuary

Protected sandbar with rich marine life, great for snorkeling.
Location:Masbate City

Catandayagan Falls

aterfall that drops into the ocean; rare and stunning.
Location:San Jacinto, Masbate

Palani Beach

Peaceful long beach perfect for swimming and relaxation.
Location:Balud, Masbate

Rodeo Masbateño Festival

Annual cowboy festival showcasing Masbate’s ranching culture.
Location: Masbate City

Halea Nature Park

Protected marine area with colorful sea life, ideal for snorkeling.
Location:Monreal, Masbate

Sorsogon

Donsol Whale Shark Interaction

World-renowned site to safely swim with whale sharks.
Location:Donsol, Sorsogon

Subic Beach

Known for pinkish sand and clear waters, a unique beach destination.
Location:Matnog, Sorsogon

Tikling Island

Private island with white sand and crystal-clear water.
Location:Matnog, Sorsogon

Juag Lagoon Marine Sanctuary

Marine sanctuary where visitors can feed and observe marine animals.
Location:Matnog, Sorsogon

Bulusan Lake

Peaceful lake at the foot of Bulusan Volcano, great for kayaking.
Location:Bulusan, Sorsogon

Bulusan Volcano Natural Park

Protected park with forests, hot springs, and wildlife.
Location:Bulusan, Sorsogon

Paguriran Island & Lagoon

A lagoon inside a rock formation surrounded by blue waters.
Location:Bacon, Sorsogon

Barcelona Church Ruins

Historic stone church, one of the oldest in Bicol.
Location:Barcelona, Sorsogon
