Our History & Culture

Brief History

The Bicol Region, also known as Region V, has a rich history influenced by pre-colonial culture, Spanish colonization, and local traditions. Early settlers were believed to be Austronesian migrants who established thriving communities near coastal areas.

During the Spanish era, churches and towns were established throughout Bicol. The region played a role in the Philippine revolution and has since evolved into a vibrant cultural and economic hub.
